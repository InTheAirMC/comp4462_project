Enter the world of Oscar Bait.

What is Oscar Bait?

Oscar Bait movies can be understood as bad movies that somehow got Oscar awards.

Using popular movie reviewing site RottenTomatoes, we technically define Oscar Bait movies as oscar-nominated movies that received a poor audience rating of Spilled in RottenTomatoes.

Oscar Bait Movies over time

Over the years, the trend of Oscar-bait movies being nominated is rising.

From 1950 to 2017, the frequency of poorly received Oscar-nominated films has notably increased.
